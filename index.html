Features



 

Error Detection

Our error detection system is based on a list of non-negotiable formal essay components and common errors, carefully compiled by several high school instructors.

 

 

Assistance

Receive automated guidance through the essay-writing process, automatically detect major errors in your paper, and easily find synonyms for thousands of words to enhance the quality of your work.

 

 

Improvement

Muzzy cannot be used as a cheating tool. Our app is designed to identify errors, not to fix them for you. Over time, this helps you improve your own skill at finding and fixing issues in your papers.
